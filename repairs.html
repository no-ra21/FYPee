Diesel Engines

On & off-site repair or complete overhaul 2 and 4 stroke diesel engines. We can provide fully serviced (to maker's specifications) cylinder heads, pistons and connecting rods to reduce downtime significantly.

Air Compressors

Repair or full recondition reciprocating or screw type air compressors. Fully recondition air compressors including overhaul, inter/discharge valves, piston rings, bearings, etc. and carry out function test before dispatch to vessel.

Purifiers

Full remanufacture of various makes & models of purifiers with fully balanced bowls and function tested in our workshop.

Hydraulic Motors

Fully recondition various makes & types of hydraulic motors & pumps. Load tested & certified in house. Japanese & European Hydraulics.

Pumps

Repair and recondition. Various types of pumps, centrifugal, screw, gear, vane types, European & Japanese makes.
