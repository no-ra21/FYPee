Shaping the future of the maritime industry through innovation and integrity.

Our Story

Ark Vision Spare & Engineering Pte Ltd, incorporated in Singapore on 20th August 1998, is a focused stockist and supplier of marine spares & equipment to shipping related establishments. Since then, we have consistently strengthened our position in the Asian market to become the largest and most dynamically developed stockist and supplier of new & reconditioned, genuine & OEM marine spares and equipment.

We operate from approximately 32,000 square feet warehouse & workshop in Tuas, Jurong, in close proximity to the Shipyards and key supporting services, eg, equipment manufacturers, machining & logistics service providers, etc. We have achieved ISO 9001 quality certification and BizSafe Level 4.

Our Mission

We are a leading marine spare parts & machinery supplier for shipping related establishments. Through the investment in our people, systems and ready stocks, we strive to supply quality & ready spare parts & machinery at cost effective prices.

Our Vision

To become an undisputed leader in the supplies and stockist of large variety of spare parts and machinery for shipowners, managers, makers & repairers.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We build trust through honest communication and responsible action.
  • Innovation: We challenge the status quo, continuously seeking better solutions.
  • Collaboration: We succeed together with our clients, partners and communities.
  • Excellence: We strive for quality in every detail, from design to delivery.
